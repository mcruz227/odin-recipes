Fries

Description

Crispy fries that you can have at breakfast, lunch, or dinner. You can make them cubed, sliced, or wedged.

ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Seasonings of your Choice

How to make

  1. Rinse and drain your cut up potatoes.
  2. Season your potatoes.
  3. Put in your air-fryer for 15 minutes (or until crispy) on 390 degrees Farenheight.
