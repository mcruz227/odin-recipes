GUACAMOLE

Description

Avocado dip typically made with mashed avocado and some lime juice, and seasoned with salt and cilantro.

ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Limes or Lime juice
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Minced Garlic
  • Cayenne Pepper

How to make

  1. In a bowl, mash up your avocados.
  2. Add your salt and your lime juice and mash.
  3. Add your diced tomatoes, fresh cilantro, and your minced garlic. Mash together.
  4. Add a pinch of cayenne pepper. MASH
  5. Serve with chips, crackers, or anything of your choosing and ENJOY.
