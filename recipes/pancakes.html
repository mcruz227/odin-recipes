Pancakes

Description

Delicious easy homemade pancakes to have for breakfast or any part of your day really.

ingredients

  • Flour
  • 2 Tablespns of white Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg

How to make

  1. Take all the dry ingredients, and combine them in a bowl..
  2. Add your wet ingredients and mix it all together.
  3. Take an oiled pan and pour the batter into the shape you want your pancake.
  4. Cook it until bubbles form. Then, FLIP!
  5. Serve with maple syrup and fresh fruit if youd like. ENJOY.
